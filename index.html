How to Play: Pig Game

The goal of Pig Game is to be the first player to reach 100 points. Players take turns rolling a dice as many times as they like, the score on the dice will be added to their score. However, if a player rolls a 1, they lose all their points for that turn, then it will move to the opponent's turn. A player can choose to hold their current score and pass the turn to the opponent to save their points. Have fun playing!! ðŸ˜Š
